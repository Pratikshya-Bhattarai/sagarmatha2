About Sagarmatha Investments

Sagarmatha Investments is a professional stock brokerage and investment advisory firm dedicated to helping clients achieve financial growth and security. Inspired by the strength and stability of Mount Everest, we combine deep market expertise with modern trading technology to provide transparent, reliable, and client-focused financial solutions.

We specialize in:

  • Stock Brokerage Services – Seamless buying and selling of shares.
  • Investment Advisory – Expert guidance for short-term and long-term wealth creation.
  • Portfolio Management – Tailored strategies to balance risk and growth.
  • Market Research – Data-driven insights to support smart investment decisions.

Our Services

  • Stock Trading (Equity Delivery & Intraday): Buying & selling company shares on NEPSE or other markets. Intraday trading for quick profits or long-term delivery trades.
  • IPO / FPO Application Handling: Easy online application for Initial Public Offerings and Further Public Offerings. Support with ASBA & CDSC accounts.
  • Portfolio Management Services (PMS): Expert-managed investment portfolio based on risk appetite. Diversification across stocks, mutual funds, bonds.
  • Mutual Funds & Bonds Trading: Access to government bonds, debentures, and mutual funds.
  • Research & Advisory: Market insights, stock analysis, buy/sell recommendations.
  • Online Trading Platforms: Mobile & web apps for trading convenience with real-time market data & charts.
  • Demat & MeroShare Support: Demat account opening & management. Assistance with share transfers, pledging, or dematerialization.

Pricing & Fees

  • Brokerage Commission: 0.27% – 0.40% (based on transaction volume)
  • Account Setup: Rs. 100 one-time + Rs. 100 yearly maintenance
  • Trading Platform: Free mobile & web access
  • Advisory & Research: Free daily updates + premium research packages

Brokerage fees as per SEBON rules:

  • Up to Rs. 50,000 → 0.40% commission
  • Rs. 50,001 – 500,000 → 0.37% commission
  • Rs. 500,001 – 2,000,000 → 0.34% commission
  • Rs. 2,000,001 – 10,000,000 → 0.30% commission
  • Above Rs. 10 million → 0.27% commission
